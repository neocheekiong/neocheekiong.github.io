Korea

Gamcheon Culture Village

The Gamcheon Culture Village has been renovated and repurposed into a tourism site. The brightly coloured buildings present an Instagrammable scene for visitors. There are also various cafes in the area for tourists to take a break in.

Eulsukdo Island

This is another Instagrammable spot with many visitors posing for photos in and around the bushes.

Taejongdae Resort Park

This park is rather touristy. You can get on the train for KRW 10,000 or walk the 4.3km trail. The waves crashing into the cliffs were a mesmerising sight.

Spider having a meal

I spotted a spider having its meal among the trees near the temple at the top of the cliffs. Apparently, there are also fireflies during September and October. We've missed them by a month.

Songdo Beach Skywalk

When we arrived at Songdo Skywalk, the sun has set and the cold winds were blowing harshly. Thank goodness I brought my down jacket!

Haedongyonggungsa Temple

Haedongyonggungsa Temple is a Buddhist temple by the rocky seaside. There is a trail leading to the beach but we did not take them. This is a low exposure experimental photo of the temple with the sun behind it. The location is good for a sunrise view if you can make it in time.

Pond at Entrance of Bulguska Temple

Bulguska Temple is a scenic spot with plenty of beautiful scenery during fall.

Bulguska Temple Complex

One of the staff members called out to some of us to follow her. She brought us to a spot along the walls and pointed this scene out to us. It seems to be right out of a movie.
